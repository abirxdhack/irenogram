The account has been limited/deactivated
----------------------------------------

If your account is displaying a "limited" or "deactivated" banner, this is a Telegram-side restriction
and Irenogram cannot bypass it. Common reasons:

- Sending spam or bulk unsolicited messages.
- Rapidly joining and leaving groups.
- Using automation in ways that violate Telegram's Terms of Service.

To appeal, use the `Telegram support bot <https://t.me/SpamBot>`_ or contact
`Telegram support <https://telegram.org/support>`_ directly.

.. warning::

    Using Irenogram to automate actions that violate
    `Telegram's Terms of Service <https://telegram.org/tos>`_ may result in a permanent ban.
    Always use the library responsibly.
